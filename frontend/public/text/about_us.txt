Ever since their inception in 2020, Intentional Offence has dominated the nu black indie slam prog garage grind death metal core scene in the mid southwest central Alberta metropolitan area.
Forged in the infernal heat of an un-air-conditioned Red Deer apartment, Intentional Offence has a raw, twisted sound that leaves listeners feeling dazed and acoustically violated.
No instrument is safe from the clutches of founding members Riley and Grant.
Those brave enough to scour the discography will find everything from mic stand and pill bottle samples to guitar abuse worthy of capital punishment.
